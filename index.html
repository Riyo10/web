About Us


Welcome to Metal Bengal, your premier destination for high-quality stainless steel, iron, and aluminum products for your residential and commercial needs. With years of experience and a passion for craftsmanship, we take pride in creating stunning and durable window, gate, stairs, railing, and roofing solutions that enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your space.

At Metal Bengal, we understand the importance of exceptional design, superior materials, and precise manufacturing techniques. That's why we employ a team of skilled artisans and technicians who are dedicated to delivering products of the highest standards. Our attention to detail and commitment to excellence ensure that each project is meticulously executed, surpassing your expectations every time.

Whether you're looking for sleek and modern stainless steel structures or the timeless elegance of iron and aluminum, we offer a wide range of customizable options to suit your unique preferences and architectural style. Our expert team will work closely with you to understand your vision, providing personalized solutions that perfectly blend functionality, aesthetics, and durability.

In addition to our expertise in metals, we also specialize in glass works, offering a comprehensive range of services to meet all your glazing requirements. From decorative glass installations to robust and energy-efficient solutions, we bring innovation and artistry to your glass projects, transforming spaces with transparency and elegance.

At Metal Bengal, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to create lasting relationships with our clients by offering exceptional customer service, timely project completion, and competitive pricing. We are committed to guiding you through every step of the process, from concept to installation, ensuring a seamless experience and outstanding results.

We invite you to visit our showroom and explore our extensive collection of designs and materials. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be delighted to assist you in finding the perfect solution for your specific needs. Experience the difference that quality craftsmanship and personalized attention can make when you choose Metal Bengal.

Trust Metal Bengal for all your stainless steel, iron, aluminum, and glass requirements.
